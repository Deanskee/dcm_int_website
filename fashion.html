    Our Process

    Here is an overview of how we approach each new collection or design.

    Plan

    • Design concept and work with the client to pinpoint the perfect manufacturer.
    • Material selection
    • Send sample to manufacturer
    • Once sample has been completed, the client will receive the product, critique as much as possible until satisfied
    • Approve sample and sign off on an agreed amount order to begin production

    Create

    • Once satisfied, there is a 50% down payment on approved order
    • Prototype production initiates
    • When production has been completed & is ready to ship we require 30% of the payment to deliver products to the client
    • Once the product has reached the client's destination, the final 20% of the order is required to complete the order

    Reveal

    • Received order verified by customer
    • Analyze feedback
    • Bring to market
